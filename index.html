Welcome to OER Bookr!

OER Bookr is a teacher's source for open educational resources (OER). Picture the Yelp of text books, a social platform for peer reviewed, open licensed text books. Teachers have the ability to review textbooks based on their experience with them, and use the reviews to devise better teaching plans for their students. The OER Bookr includes several features that are available to registered users, including CRUD (Create, Read, Update, and Delete) features.

Who Should Use OER Bookr?

OER Bookr is a teacher's best friend. When devising their teaching curriculm, teachers often have difficulties deciding that supplementing textbook materials should be used for their students. However, using OER Bookr, teachers have the flexibility to find the most suitable, peer reviewed resources to help them design courses and stimulate student learning.

Alternatively, students who wish to learn more about various academic subjects can use OER Bookr to choose additional textbooks for their benefits. Students can choose based on the book's review, availability, and difficulty, using the teachers' reviews!

Why Choose Bookr?

OER Bookr is friendly to use! It is similar to Yelp in terms of function and purpose, thus it is proven to be user friendly. Users can use the app from desktop computers, tablets, or smartphones. The design layout is simple and easy to follow in order to create a positive experience for our users.

OER Bookr is the only app on the net that is comprehensively supported by a team of developers! We will constantly be updating and maintaining the website, ensuring that users will have access to their needed information at all times. In addition, we will be updating the application with new features over time.

How do I Get Started?

For starter, click the register button at the top right of the website. You'll be prompted to enter various information. Once the process is completed, you'll be able to navigate OER Bookr as a registered user, and that will allows you to add, delete, modify, and do other cool things with your review.

Review for each textbook contains its title, author, publisher, license, and reviews. The User Interface to modify these information is clear and easy to understand. If you need help navigating the application, please refer to the video at top of the page for additional help.
